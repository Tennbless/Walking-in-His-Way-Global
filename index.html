About Our Ministry

Serving God's Kingdom with Faith, Hope, and Love. We are a global ministry committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and transforming lives through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and Spirit-led service.

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ across the globe, transforming lives through the power of God's Word and empowering believers to walk in His way with purpose and conviction. We are dedicated to making disciples, strengthening faith, and building God's kingdom through authentic relationships, Spirit-filled worship, and compassionate service to our communities and the world.

Our Vision

A world where every person encounters the transformative love of Christ, where communities are restored through the power of the Gospel, and where God's kingdom advances through faithful disciples walking in His-Way. We envision a global movement of believers who are empowered by the Holy Spirit, equipped with biblical truth, and committed to living out their faith with excellence and integrity.

Our Values

Grounded in Scripture, led by the Holy Spirit, committed to prayer, dedicated to service, and united in love. We believe in the power of authentic community, Spirit-filled ministry, and the transformative work of God in every life. Our values include biblical integrity, cultural relevance, compassionate service, prophetic worship, and unwavering faith in God's promises and purposes.

Our Ministries

Discover opportunities to serve, grow, and connect through our diverse ministry programs. Each ministry is designed to meet you where you are and help you develop into the person God has called you to be.

Women's Ministry - Women of Poise and Presence

Empowering women to discover their God-given identity, purpose, and potential. Through fellowship, mentorship, and biblical teaching, we cultivate women of grace, strength, and spiritual elegance who carry the presence of God with confidence and authenticity. Our ministry provides a safe space for women to grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and develop into influential leaders who impact their families, communities, and the world for Christ.

Men's Ministry - Men of Distinction

Calling men to rise up as godly leaders, faithful husbands, devoted fathers, and men of integrity. We challenge men to pursue excellence, embrace accountability, and walk in their God-given authority with humility and strength. Through mentorship, brotherhood, and biblical discipleship, we equip men to be distinguished in character, purpose-driven in their calling, and unwavering in their commitment to Christ and His kingdom.

Children's Ministry - Jubilee (Ages 0-12)

Creating a joyful, safe, and engaging environment where children encounter God's love and learn biblical truths. Through age-appropriate worship, interactive lessons, and creative activities, we lay a foundation of faith that will sustain children throughout their lives. Our dedicated team nurtures each child's spiritual growth, celebrates their unique gifts, and helps them develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ filled with wonder, joy, and jubilation.

Young Adults - Aflame In Motion (Ages 18-35)

Igniting passionate, purpose-driven young adults who are on fire for God and making a difference in the world. We provide relevant teaching, authentic community, and practical opportunities to live out your faith boldly. Our ministry empowers young adults to navigate life's challenges with biblical wisdom, pursue their God-given dreams with excellence, and be a generation that moves in the power of the Holy Spirit, impacting culture and advancing God's kingdom.

Pathways to Growth

Intentional spiritual journeys designed to help you discover who you are in Christ, develop your faith, and fulfill your divine purpose. These pathways provide structure, support, and biblical guidance for your walk with God.

Lightwork - Discovering Your Purpose

Illuminate your path and discover God's unique purpose for your life. Lightwork is a transformative journey that helps you identify your spiritual gifts, recognize your calling, and understand how God has uniquely designed you to shine His light in the world. Through biblical exploration, personal reflection, and Spirit-led guidance, you'll gain clarity on your mission and learn how to walk confidently in your God-given purpose, becoming a beacon of hope and truth in your sphere of influence.

Foundation - New Believer's Journey

Build a solid foundation in your new faith with biblical essentials and discipleship basics. This comprehensive pathway is designed for new believers or those wanting to strengthen their spiritual foundation. You'll learn core Christian doctrines, develop healthy spiritual habits, understand prayer and worship, discover the importance of community, and establish patterns that will sustain your faith for a lifetime. Our experienced mentors will guide you through Scripture, answer your questions, and help you grow strong in Christ.

Ascend - Leadership Development

Rise to your full potential as a leader in God's kingdom through intensive training, mentorship, and practical ministry experience. Ascend equips emerging leaders with biblical leadership principles, strategic thinking, spiritual formation, and ministry skills. You'll learn how to lead with integrity, influence with wisdom, build effective teams, navigate challenges, and multiply your impact. This pathway is for those who sense God's call to leadership and are ready to develop their gifts and abilities to serve His church and advance His kingdom.
