About Our Ministry

Serving God's Kingdom with Faith, Hope, and Love. We are a global ministry committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and transforming lives through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and Spirit-led service.

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ across the globe, transforming lives through the power of God's Word and empowering believers to walk in His way with purpose and conviction. We are dedicated to making disciples, strengthening faith, and building God's kingdom through authentic relationships, Spirit-filled worship, and compassionate service to our communities and the world.

Our Vision

A world where every person encounters the transformative love of Christ, where communities are restored through the power of the Gospel, and where God's kingdom advances through faithful disciples walking in His-Way. We envision a global movement of believers who are empowered by the Holy Spirit, equipped with biblical truth, and committed to living out their faith with excellence and integrity.

Our Values

Grounded in Scripture, led by the Holy Spirit, committed to prayer, dedicated to service, and united in love. We believe in the power of authentic community, Spirit-filled ministry, and the transformative work of God in every life. Our values include biblical integrity, cultural relevance, compassionate service, prophetic worship, and unwavering faith in God's promises and purposes.

Our Ministries

Discover opportunities to serve, grow, and connect through our diverse ministry programs.

Women's Ministry – Women of Poise and Presence

Empowering women to discover their God-given identity, purpose, and potential. Through fellowship, mentorship, and biblical teaching, we cultivate women of grace, strength, and spiritual elegance who carry the presence of God with confidence and authenticity.

Men's Ministry – Men of Distinction

Calling men to rise up as godly leaders, faithful husbands, devoted fathers, and men of integrity. We challenge men to pursue excellence, embrace accountability, and walk in their God-given authority with humility and strength.

Children's Ministry – Jubilee (Ages 0–12)

Creating a joyful, safe, and engaging environment where children encounter God's love and learn biblical truths. Through age-appropriate worship, interactive lessons, and creative activities, we lay a foundation of faith that will sustain children throughout their lives.

Young Adults – Aflame In Motion (Ages 18–35)

Igniting passionate, purpose-driven young adults who are on fire for God and making a difference in the world. We provide relevant teaching, authentic community, and practical opportunities to live out your faith boldly.

Pathways to Growth

Intentional spiritual journeys designed to help you discover who you are in Christ, develop your faith, and fulfill your divine purpose.

Lightwork – Discovering Your Purpose

Illuminate your path and discover God's unique purpose for your life. Lightwork is a transformative journey that helps you identify your spiritual gifts, recognize your calling, and understand how God has uniquely designed you to shine His light in the world.

Foundation – New Believer's Journey

Build a solid foundation in your new faith with biblical essentials and discipleship basics. Designed for new believers or those wanting to strengthen their spiritual foundation, covering core Christian doctrines, spiritual habits, prayer, and community.

Ascend – Leadership Development

Rise to your full potential as a leader in God's kingdom through intensive training, mentorship, and practical ministry experience. Ascend equips emerging leaders with biblical leadership principles, strategic thinking, and spiritual formation.
