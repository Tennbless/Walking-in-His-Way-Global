Who We Serve
Pathways – The Family Table | Walking in His-Way Global

Pathways – The Family Table exists to serve every person in our community who is facing hardship, food insecurity, or a need for essential resources. We open our doors without judgment, without prerequisites, and without barriers.

Families & Children

We stand alongside families navigating financial hardship, job loss, or transitions that place daily necessities out of reach. From nutritious food to hygiene essentials, we ensure children never go without the basics they need to be healthy, present, and thriving. When a family's foundational needs are met, every member has the capacity to grow.

Seniors

Our elders have given so much to their communities — they deserve to receive with dignity. We provide seniors with access to pantry staples, fresh produce, personal care items, and a network of care that sees and values them. No senior in our community should worry about where their next meal will come from.

Single Mothers

Single mothers carry extraordinary weight. Pathways offers practical, tangible support — food, care packages, and community connection — so that they do not carry that weight alone. We honor their strength while lightening their load.

Faith-Based Outreach

Rooted in the mission of Walking in His-Way Global, Pathways extends its reach through partnerships with local churches, ministries, and faith communities. Together, we multiply our capacity to serve, bringing resources and hope to neighborhoods and households that need it most.

Our Commitment

We serve every person with dignity, confidentiality, and genuine care. No documentation barriers. No shame. Just a community committed to ensuring that every person — regardless of circumstance — has access to the basics of a stable and nourished life.

Our Services
Pathways – The Family Table | Walking in His-Way Global

At Pathways – The Family Table, our services are built around one principle: that every person deserves access to the resources that make a dignified, stable life possible. We provide hands-on, practical support that addresses physical, emotional, and spiritual needs.

Food Pantry

Our food pantry offers regular distributions of nutritious, non-perishable food items alongside fresh produce when available. We stock staples that form the foundation of healthy meals — grains, proteins, vegetables, canned goods, and household basics. Families may visit on a consistent schedule and receive what they need to keep their household fed and nourished.

Care Packages

Our care packages are thoughtfully assembled kits containing toiletries, hygiene products, and personal care essentials. Each package is curated to meet the real, everyday needs of individuals and families — from toothpaste and soap to feminine care and household supplies. No one should have to choose between food and hygiene.

Wraparound Community Resources

Beyond our direct programs, Pathways serves as a bridge to a broader network of community resources. We connect families to housing support, employment assistance, health services, and educational opportunities — helping them move from a moment of crisis toward long-term stability and growth.

Spiritual & Emotional Support

As a faith-rooted organization under Walking in His-Way Global, we believe that serving the whole person means attending to heart and spirit alongside material needs. Prayer, encouragement, and compassionate presence are always available to those who seek them.

How to Access Services

To receive services from Pathways – The Family Table, simply reach out through our assistance request form or contact us directly. There is no lengthy application process. We ask only that you come as you are — and we will meet you there.

Food Pantry
Pathways – The Family Table | Walking in His-Way Global

The Pathways Food Pantry is the cornerstone of our food security mission. We believe that no family in our community should face hunger — and through our pantry, we work every week to make that belief a reality.

What We Provide

Our pantry stocks a wide variety of nutritious, non-perishable food items alongside fresh produce when available through donations and community partnerships. Families can expect to receive:

  • Canned proteins — beans, tuna, chicken, and lentils
  • Grains — rice, pasta, oats, and bread
  • Canned and jarred vegetables and fruits
  • Soups, stews, and ready-to-prepare meal items
  • Cooking oils, sauces, and seasonings
  • Fresh produce when available through partnerships
  • Baby food and formula for households with infants

How to Access the Food Pantry

Accessing the Pathways Food Pantry is simple. Submit a request through our assistance form or visit us in person. There is no extensive application, no income verification required, and no barriers to receiving care. We ask only that families come forward so we can serve them.

Distribution Schedule

Our food pantry operates on a regular distribution schedule. Please reach out through the assistance request form to receive current hours and distribution dates, as schedules may vary seasonally.

How You Can Help

The Food Pantry is sustained by the generosity of our community. You can support it by donating non-perishable food items, organizing a food drive, volunteering to sort and distribute, or making a financial gift designated to the Food Pantry Program.

Care Packages
Pathways – The Family Table | Walking in His-Way Global

The Pathways Care Package program addresses a critical gap in community support: the essential personal care and hygiene items that food assistance programs don't cover — but that every person absolutely needs to live with health and dignity.

What Is Included

Each care package is thoughtfully assembled to meet the real everyday needs of individuals and families. Standard packages typically include:

  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, and floss
  • Bar soap, body wash, and hand soap
  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Deodorant and personal hygiene items
  • Feminine hygiene products
  • Razors and shaving supplies
  • Lotion, lip balm, and skin care basics
  • Laundry detergent and household cleaning supplies
  • Paper products — toilet paper, tissues, and paper towels

Customized to Your Needs

Care packages can be customized based on household size, the ages of family members, and specific needs. When you submit a request, please let us know about your household so we can ensure your package is tailored appropriately — including supplies for infants, children, seniors, or individuals with specific health considerations.

How to Request a Care Package

Requesting a care package is as simple as completing our assistance form. A member of our team will coordinate pickup, delivery, or distribution based on your circumstances and our current schedule.

Support This Program

You can support our care package program by donating hygiene products and household items, volunteering to assemble packages, or making a financial gift designated to the Care Package program.

Community Kitchen
Pathways – The Family Table | Walking in His-Way Global

The Pathways Community Kitchen is currently in active development — and it represents one of the most exciting chapters in our story. It is not just a place to cook meals; it is a space where community, dignity, and nourishment come together under one roof.

What the Community Kitchen Will Offer

Once fully operational, the Pathways Community Kitchen will provide:

  • Regular hot meal preparation and service for community members in need
  • Cooking education classes for families — budgeting, nutrition, and meal planning
  • Culinary skills training for community members seeking employment pathways
  • A shared commercial kitchen space for small food entrepreneurs and community programs
  • Holiday and special event meal services for seniors and isolated individuals
  • Integration with the Pathways Food Pantry to reduce food waste and maximize impact

Why It Matters

A community kitchen transforms the act of feeding people from a transaction into a relationship. When neighbors gather, cook, and eat together — connections form, skills transfer, and the isolation of poverty begins to break down. The Pathways Community Kitchen is designed to be both a resource and a destination.

Current Status

We are currently in the build-out phase of our community kitchen. Our team is securing equipment, establishing health and safety compliance, and recruiting culinary volunteers and partners. We anticipate a phased opening in the coming months.

How to Support the Kitchen

You can support the Community Kitchen build-out by making a financial gift designated to this program, donating commercial kitchen equipment, volunteering construction or renovation skills, or partnering with us as an organizational sponsor.

Community Market
Pathways – The Family Table | Walking in His-Way Global

The Pathways Community Market is our boldest vision — a community-owned, affordable grocery market that ensures every family has access to fresh, nutritious food at prices they can afford. It is how we permanently address the root causes of food insecurity in our neighborhood.

The Problem We Are Solving

Many of the families we serve live in or near food deserts — geographic areas without reliable access to affordable, healthy groceries. This is not simply a matter of income; it is a matter of infrastructure. Without a nearby market offering healthy options at accessible prices, the cycle of poor nutrition and financial strain perpetuates itself. The Community Market is our answer to that cycle.

What the Community Market Will Be

  • An affordable, community-accessible grocery market with fresh produce, proteins, and pantry staples
  • A dignified shopping experience designed with the community, not just for the community
  • A source of local employment and economic opportunity for community members
  • An anchor institution that stabilizes the neighborhood and attracts complementary services
  • Integrated with the Pathways Food Pantry and Community Kitchen ecosystem

Community Ownership Model

True to the values of Walking in His-Way Global, the Community Market will be built on a model of community stewardship. Community members will have a voice in its operations, its product offerings, and its ongoing development — ensuring it always serves the people it was created for.

How to Be Part of This Vision

The Community Market is a long-term vision that requires community investment. You can be part of making it a reality through financial gifts designated to this initiative, strategic partnerships, land or facility contributions, or by spreading the word and building community support.
