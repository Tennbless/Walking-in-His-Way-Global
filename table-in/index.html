Our Calling

A seat for everyone who needs one

Table-In™ is more than a feeding program — it is a sacred extended pathway rooted in the belief that every human being deserves to sit at a table with dignity, nourishment, and belonging.

Born from the heart of Walking in His Way Global Ministry™, Table-In™ reaches those the world too often overlooks: the single parent raising children alone, the orphan who has never known a family meal, the widow who eats in silence, the child who goes to school hungry.

We are not simply handing out food. We are restoring the table — a place of community, of healing, of God’s provision made tangible.

Single Mothers

Nourishment, community, and support for mothers carrying the weight of the household.

Single Fathers

Honoring fathers who show up every day — giving them rest, a hot meal, and a community.

Children & Orphans

Every child deserves a full plate. We prioritize the youngest among us with fresh, wholesome food.

Widows & Singles

No one should eat alone. We create tables of belonging for those who have lost or have none.

The Innovation

What if the hands that plant the seed could sit at the same table where it is served? What if those we feed could grow the food that feeds them — and in doing so, discover purpose, livelihood, and community? This is the Table-In™ Pathway. Never before done quite like this.

Why This Has Never Been Done Quite This Way

Most farm-to-table programs supply food to those in need. Table-In™ does something radically different: we invite the people we serve into every stage of the cycle — not as recipients but as participants, co-creators, and ultimately providers. A single mother plants tomatoes in month one; by month four, she is teaching others and earning income from the surplus.

The pathway doesn’t just feed bodies — it restores agency, community, and the deeply human joy of providing for others. This is Table-In™: where the table is not just set for you, but built by you.

The Process

How Table-In™ works

01

Register or Refer

Single parents, widows, orphans, and individuals in need can self-register or be referred by a church, school, or community partner. There is no means test — just a need for a table.

02

Come to the Table

Participants are welcomed at scheduled community meal gatherings — warm, farm-fresh meals served with dignity. No queues, no stigma, just fellowship.

03

Grow with Us

Those who wish can join our training garden program — learning to grow food, harvest it, cook it, and eventually earn from the surplus. Every season brings a new participant into the full circle.
